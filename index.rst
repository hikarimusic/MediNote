**Welcome to MediNote!**
========================
**MediNote** is an online resource for reviewing basic and clinical medicine, 
created by Yeu-Guang Tung (董宇光) from National Taiwan University School of Medicine. 
It is specifically tailored for individuals preparing for medical licensing examinations in Taiwan, the United States, and Japan.
